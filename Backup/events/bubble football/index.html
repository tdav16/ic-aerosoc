Bubble Football

Saturday 5th November 2016

41 S Africa Rd, White City, London W12 7RW


AeroSoc is organizing its first Bubble Football event!

The event will take place on Saturday 5th of November in Shepherds Bush where, for an hour, 20 people will have the opportunity to try to play football whilst being inside a ball.
